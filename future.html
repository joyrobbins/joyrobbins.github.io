Take a glimpse of future projects I'm dreaming up!

"Code With Friends" App

This web app will allow two players to work together to move a character through obstacles that increase in complexity on each progressive level. The best part? Each player has to take a turn coding to move the character through the obstacles. Coding is great. Coding with Friends is even better!

A Women and LGBTQ+ Friendly Coding Forum

One of the most intimidating parts of learning to code for me is interacting with others on coding forums. I'd like to collaborate with other women technologists to create a more friendly, inviting forum for women, LGBTQ+, and other people who may not feel comfortable in existing coding forum spaces.

"Walk With Me" LGBTQ+ Oral History & Travel App

This app combines the best of oral history and walking tours! Open the app to walk in the footsteps of LGBTQ+ elders and activists as they describe their personal experiences in the very space in which you're walking. Can't travel? No problem. Just take a walk wherever you'd like and listen as history unfolds in your ears.
